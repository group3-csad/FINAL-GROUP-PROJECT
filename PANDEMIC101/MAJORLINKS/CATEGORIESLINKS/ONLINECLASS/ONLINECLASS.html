15 Tips To Survive
Online Class





The Pandemic has greatly affected us students, leading us to adjust into the online setup of learning. Some of us may have coped already in this change, but for the others you are clueless and might be asking "What will help me boost my excitement and be productive to learn during the pandemic?" With that, this blog will show you some netiquettes to help you enjoy online class and have the school vibes even at home.
