SAFETY MEETS STYLE

Fashion isn't cancelled for the rest of the year, at least not for Heart Evangelista. Heart Evangelista goes viral in Tiktok because of her fashion sense, amidst this pandemic she still pull off her outfits so well.


On Instgaram, the Kapuso actress and style icon shared how she plans to continue serving runway-ready looks while protecting herself from the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19). Using the hashtag #TheNewNormalWithLoveMarie, Heart shared some behind-the-scenes pics from an at-home fashion shoot, as well as some statement pieces that she plans to pair with gloves, face shields and personal protective equipment (PPE).
