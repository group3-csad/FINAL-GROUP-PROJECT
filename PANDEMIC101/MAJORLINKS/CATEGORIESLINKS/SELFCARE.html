WonderSelf:
Loving Who You Are














Get up and do some stretching, this will help you be fit even at home and give you energy through out the day.




Take baths to help you relax and make sure you are free from germs and viruses especially when from outside the house.



Pandemic doesn't mean we limit our social life. We need regular talks and catch ups with our friends online. Our friends are our support and we can lean on when we encounter problems or even if we just want to have a conversation with someone.





During this time, making time for ourselves is important. Watching movies can help us relax and help us release our stress and distract us from work, school or even the world.





Looking at the brighter side, this is the best time to do the things we love while at home. We can do and explore new hobbies and activities to make the most of our time everyday.





Eating healthy food is one of the best way to take care of ourselves. Not only does it boost our immune system but also can help us become fit. Cravings are totally okay as long as it has limitations.





Let's always make time and focus on ourselves. Learning to be confident and appreciating what we have is the best care we can give ourselves.
