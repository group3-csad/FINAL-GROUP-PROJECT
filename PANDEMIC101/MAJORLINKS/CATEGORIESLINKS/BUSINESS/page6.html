MAKE YOUR OWN HOMEMADE MILKTEA

For the past few years, Milktea has been one of the most popular beverages in the Philippines. There are milktea shops everywhere. Different brands, based teas, flavors, types of milk, fruit combinations and etc. Because of the pandemic especially during ECQs, going out and buying milkteas from your favorite milktea place can be a little challenging. So, above is a simple and easy way of making a home-made classic Miltea so you can enjoy your favorite beverage from the comfort of your own home.


Milktea or Boba Tea is a beverage found in mainly in South East Asian countries with its main ingredients, Milk and Tea. There are various ways and flavors of Milkteas depending on the culture and taste buds of the people in a country.

Ingredients

for 1 serving

  • 3 to 5 Black Tea Bags
  • 1/4 cup quick cooking boba (pearls)
  • 1 Tbs Honey
  • 2 Tbs sweet condensed Milk
  • 2 Tbs Milk
