Small Businesses of our CSAD friends

Building yourself different opportunities even outside of your field is great for oneself. Passion cannot only be found on the field of our choice or start after graduation. As students making extra money not only for oursleves but also to help lessen the burden of our parents. Here are a few of Online Shops by our CSAD friends that started during the pandemic.

Baked Satisfaction

Baked Satisfaction is a online pastry shop located in Pasig City. Their best sellers are their cheesecakes, blueberry and cookies and cream; cheese roll bread, and banana bread. They also make custom occational cakes. Thier socials are handled by one of our own, Ashley Grabato.
