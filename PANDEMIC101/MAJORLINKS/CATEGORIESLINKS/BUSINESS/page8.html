8 Simple ways to show your support for small online Businesses

Small and local businesses creates job opportunities, unique experiences and unites a community. Show your support to small and local businesses online of your friends or within your community is important for their brand growth especially during this time in the pandemic. So, here are 8 ways to show your support.

1. Visit their page or Websites

Engage with their pages and websites. The algorithm of different platforms may be different but there is one thing it all has in common. "More Engagement = More Reach". The traffic status helps them to be reached by other brands.

2. Share their Posts

Share contents. If they have announcements for an upcomming sale, or event share it. Help spread the word.

3. Like and Comment

Interaction is very important. Like, comment, and follow posts and accounts of small businesses for them to reach more people.

4. Tag a friend

Tell your friends! Recommendations are more likely to be entertained especially from people who are close to us. If they are looking for something unique, that can't be found on commercial stores, tag them on local and small business accounts!

5. Give them a Shout Out

If you have a great expirience with a shop, spread the word! Send them messages to show your appreciation. It can be done publicly or privately either would most likely be welcome.

6. Post a Picture of their product/service

Be their "brand ambassador". Post pictures of you with their product and tag them. It would encourage others to purchase something from their store.

7. Leave a Review

Giving reviews can both help the brand and the customers. You can help the brand by suggesting new to help their service or giving reviews to give more description for the expectations for future customers. Add comments and photos. Give an honest and nice review, it will make their day!.

8. Purchase Something

Place an Order. It doesn't matter how much, or how many your orders are. At the other end of that order what they would see is you showing your support by buying something. Show them that you value their work and passion.

"When you support a small business, You are supporting a dream."
